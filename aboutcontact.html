About me

I am from Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, and I am a second year student in Addis Ababa Institute Of Technology. In high school I was one of compitative kids and pay attenstion in the class room. My friends and I myself spend our time together. After completing high school I decied to study Engineering and I joined Addis Ababa University, Addis Ababa Institute Of Technology. And after pre-engineering programs that the university gave us I originally had no idea what I was going into study. After some research I decided to study Software engineering even though it is not easy I am so happy that I made a decision. and I am now a second year student.

I am participating in different fields of Volunteering especially that are related to my subject of study. I am a volunteer at Ethiopian Space Science Society. And I recently apply to be a volunteer in United Nation Organization. The Benefits of Volunteering People volunteer including myself for a wide variety of reasons, particularly wanting to help others. Some people are uncomfortable with the notion that a volunteer "benefits" from doing volunteer work. Traditionally, volunteer work is seen as a form of charity based on unselfishness and altruism.

I believe mindfulness in the college is key to success - a tenet I live out through my interests in sports, reading, gardening, and painting. The goal I’m currently striving for is to make myself better than who I was yesterday, to live in by starting the changes with myself. Sure, I’ve had bad experiences in my life too, but this is exactly what made me the way I am now: grateful, full of love, with a desire to study well because it will help me become a successful person in future and have a high quality of life. I believe that it is manifesting day by day and I feel even more responsibility for what I do and where I go. With all I already have, I know that I’m on the right path and I will do my best to inspire others to live the way they feel like living as well.
You can follow my current work on GitHub. In my free time, I love spending time with my family and my friends. Thank you so much for your attension and time.
