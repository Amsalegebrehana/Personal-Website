About me

I am from Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, and I am a second year student in Addis Ababa Institute Of Technology. In high school I was one of compitative kids and pay attenstion in the class room. My friends and I myself spend our time together. After completing high school I decied to study Engineering and I joined Addis Ababa University, Addis Ababa Institute Of Technology. And after pre-engineering programs that the university gave us I originally had no idea what I was going to study. After some research I decided to study Software engineering even though it is not easy I am so happy that I made a decision.

I am participating in different activities such as working on projects and Volunteering. My most recent project aims to reduce maternal deaths. The project is based on Robotics and the Internet of Things. We have a very successful prototype that can change the lives of many women and families, especially those living in sub-Saharan African countries. The Benefits of this project goes to 2/3 of women who dies every year during pregnancy complications that can be controlled. The application that is connected to an elastic band sensor will help a lot of people including physicians, who are loaded with a lot of working hours, and most importantly could help decrease maternal death rate with a comfortable environment and user-friendly material.

Other than this, I am working on having quality education with my friends. I believe mindfulness in the college is key to success - a tenet I live out through my interests in sports, reading, gardening, and painting. The goal I’m currently striving for is to make myself better than who I was yesterday, to live in by starting the changes with myself. Sure, I’ve had bad experiences in my life too, but this is exactly what made me the way I am now: grateful, full of love, with a desire to study well because it will help me become a successful person in future and have a high quality of life. I believe that it is manifesting day by day and I feel even more responsibility for what I do and where I go.

With all, I already have, I know that I’m on the right path. And I will do my best to inspire others to live the way they like.
You can follow my current work on GitHub. In my free time, I love spending time with my family and my friends. Thank you so much for your attension and time.
Thank you for reading.
