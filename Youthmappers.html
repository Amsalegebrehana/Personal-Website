Youth Mappers Ethiopia  

Mapping the place in Ethiopia that Google map can not reach

Last updated on April 10, 2020 5 min read
portfolio

YouthMappers Ethiopia is a program that aims to cultivate a new generation of leaders in the field of open geospatial data and technologies, with the purpose of creating through them resilient communities of the future to create a map that include every places starting from small areas to all main regions of Ethiopia. This program focus on youths. The program first give training for those who are interested and the training is at lasts at least 2 to 4 days. And the teachers come from different areas of the world.This mapping project can help the country in differetn means. For instance

  • YouthMappers chapters are mapping around the world to help provide fundamental spatial data that supports responders and aid organizations.

  • It can solve Water problem by indicating areas that water can be found for the Socitey specially around country sides

  • It helps orginaiztions to check their compound this specially helps in organazations like Universities.

  • Indicates places that are avaliable for different purpose like agriculture.

  • FOOD SECURITY - Help people map their region to support rural farming, gender, healthcare and artisan communities.

Capitalizing on web-based open geospatial technologies, and a network of universities around the globe, their mission is to cultivate a generation of young leaders to create resilient communities and to define their world by mapping it.

The need for geospatial data goes beyond the urgency of disaster response or planning. YouthMappers are also contributing time and talents to creating data in places of extreme poverty, where USAID works, to help create fundamental data for programming that addresses chronic development problems.
