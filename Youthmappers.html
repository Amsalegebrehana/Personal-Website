Information Technology For Youth Community  

Helping each other with what we have for better development

Last updated on April 10, 2020 5 min read
portfolio

Approximately 50 youth that live in Gullele mostly Woreda 5 are participating in a Wetat maekel Information Technology For Youth Community Club aimed at promoting the importance of ICTs as a tool for the empowerment of youth that are highschool students and above. This program teachs students by other unoversity students in the field of ICT who are willing to share their knowledge. The program first give training for those who are interested and the training is atleast lasts 2 weeks to 4 weeks. And the teachers come from different areas.This mapping project can help the country in different means. For instance

  • Equipping deprived and underprivileged children in terms of information technology and communication

  • Strengthen the capacity of youth students and women in rural areas on the use of computer

  • Initiate programs Computer holiday in secondary schools to train the best students in computer usage.

  • Promote access to information technology and communication and computer usage by creating a access center of IT

  • Increase the engagement of socioeconomic disadvantaged students, girls, and other underrepresented minorities in computer science.

In a social context or access to technology is limited to people living in urban areas, we have trained over one years young in the field of ICT for the youth not to face the challenges we face and our investigation to date we have trained almost 50 and above youth. Access to computers is not the prerogative of all social strata in our country; so we colaborate with wetat meakelat(Centers for the youth) that will promote access to ICTs of young people, and other disadvantaged young people for free training in computer science and computer literacy.
