Youth Mappers Ethiopia  

Mapping the place in Ethiopia that Google map can not reach

Last updated on April 10, 2020 5 min read

Youth Mappers Ethiopia is a program that aims to create a map that include every places starting from small areas to all main regions of Ethiopia. This program focus on youths. The program first give training for those who are interested and the training is at lasts at least 2 to 4 days. And the teachers come from different areas of the world.This mapping project can help the country in differetn means. For instance

  • It can solve Water problem by indicating areas that water can be found for the Socitey specially around country sides
  • It helps orginaiztions to check their compound this specially helps in organazations like Universities.
  • Indicates places that are avaliable for different purpose like agriculture.
