I am Amsale Gebrehana, second year Software engineering student in Addis Ababa University . Energetic and passionate college student working towards a BSc in Software engineering at Addis Ababa Institute Of Technology. Aiming to use my knowledge of programing, web development, and related area to make my self a good programmer and web developer. I can say that I am a responsible and a hard-working student. Moreover, being a sociable person, I have many friends since I like to communicate with people and get to know new interesting individuals. I enjoy my time at school: it is really nice to study, the students are very friendly and ready to help. The atmosphere cannot but make me want to go there every time. I like to receive and deal with challenging tasks. I am a very enthusiastic student and I think this is a strong point of mine.

Growing up as the first child in my family, I was a vital participant in a thing I did govern, in the University Collage I did choose. It's family. It's society. And often, it's chaos. You participate by letting go of the small stuff, not expecting order and perfection, and facing the unexpected with confidence, optimism, and preparedness. My family experience taught me to face a serendipitous world with confidence. I wanted to become a board-certified developer. Basically, I would get to do what I love for the rest of my life. …But it occurred to me that, while my desired occupation was decided, my true goal in life was still to become best version of myself.
